Connect Schwab to Portsie

Portsie uses the Schwab API to securely read your portfolio data. The setup takes just a few minutes.

What you'll need:
  • An existing Charles Schwab brokerage account
  • A free Schwab developer account (same login as your brokerage)

How it works

  1. Create a free developer account at developer.schwab.com using your Schwab brokerage login.
  2. Register a new app in the developer portal — Portsie's setup wizard will give you the exact settings to use.
  3. Wait for Schwab to approve your app (typically 1–3 business days).
  4. Paste your App Key and Secret into Portsie and click Connect.

Ready to get started?

Log in to Portsie and the setup wizard will guide you through each step.
